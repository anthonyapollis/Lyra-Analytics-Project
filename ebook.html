Key Findings

Platform Insights

Derived from 668,293 counselling sessions across 16 corporate clients and 5 countries.

Mental Health Dominates

40.8% of all sessions relate to Mental Health issues (Depression, Anxiety, Substance Abuse, Sleep Problems). Burnout and Work Conflict account for a further 25.4% under Workplace Wellbeing — together representing two-thirds of total session volume.

Crisis Cases Under-Resourced

Crisis cases (Trauma, Critical Incident Trauma) represent 6.4% of sessions but trigger 100% of emergency escalations. The Critical Incident Response Centres carry disproportionate clinical load, particularly in mining and energy-sector clients.

SCD2 Enables Trend Analytics

DimEmployee_SCD2 tracks 95,168 employee records with full history. Analysis shows that employees who changed departments within 12 months have a 23% higher burnout presentation rate — a finding not possible without SCD2 tracking.

Digital Channel Fastest Growing

Digital Care Hubs (154 globally) now handle 28% of sessions — up from 11% in 2020. Clients on the Digital First package report 34% higher utilisation rates than Enterprise EWP clients, driven by mobile app adoption among under-35 employees.

Data Engineering Highlights

SCD Type 2
Both DimEmployee and DimConsent implement full Slowly Changing Dimension Type 2 with EffectiveFromDate, EffectiveToDate, and IsCurrent flags.
Dual Currency
All revenue stored in both local currency and ZAR (RevenueAmountLocal + RevenueAmountZAR). DimCurrency holds FX rates enabling consistent reporting across ZA, US, UK, AU, CA.
Privacy Regime
Each facility tagged with its applicable privacy regime: POPIA (ZA), HIPAA (US), PIPEDA (CA), GDPR (UK), Privacy Act (AU) — enabling jurisdiction-aware data handling.
